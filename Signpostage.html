Find the Right Support Services

Below are trusted resources to help parents, carers, and professionals find support and guidance for managing childhood obesity and promoting healthy lifestyles.

NHS – Childhood Obesity

The NHS provides comprehensive advice on childhood obesity, including symptoms, causes, treatment options, and when to seek medical help.

NHS – Healthier Families Campaign

This site offers fun and practical tips for families to eat well and move more, including meal ideas and physical activity suggestions for children.

UK Government – Childhood Obesity Plan

Details government strategies and policies aimed at reducing obesity rates among children and young people in England.

NICE Guidelines – Obesity Prevention (PH47)

Clinical guidelines from the National Institute for Health and Care Excellence (NICE) on the prevention of obesity in adults and children.

NHS – Mental Health Support for Children

Support services for children's mental health, which can often be impacted by obesity or related issues such as body image and self-esteem.

Note: All external links open in a new tab. Please consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice.
